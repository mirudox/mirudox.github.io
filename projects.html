Family Tree Archive

Using genealogy software from Synium Software (a paid platform I did not build), I have spent years piecing together my family's lineage, past 7,500 members so far and still growing. It runs both directions: generations upon generations up the paternal and maternal lines, and just as far sideways through siblings, their descendants, and the in-law families connected along the way. None of it existed on paper. It came from elders who still remembered it, gathered through phone calls and messages, and from people who helped connect me to them. Genealogy has no real finish line, and I expect this will eventually be carried forward by someone else in the family, long after I've added what I can. Once shared, it will stay private to family, not published publicly.
