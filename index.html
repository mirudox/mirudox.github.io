The Story

Who I Am

I have never followed a conventional path into the technology industry. Growing up in Mumbai in a family of engineers, I was surrounded by technology from an early age. Computers, home theatre systems, networking equipment, projectors, displays, audio systems, and consumer electronics were part of everyday life. My father, an electronics engineer, encouraged me to explore how things worked rather than simply use them. Over time, that curiosity expanded beyond audio and video into networking, streaming, software, automation, artificial intelligence, consumer technology, and the broader world of connected systems.

My academic journey took me through Computer Science and later an integrated B.Ed.-M.Ed. programme. While it was not a traditional route into engineering, each stage contributed something valuable. Computer Science provided a technical foundation and strengthened my understanding of software and networking, while Education developed my ability to communicate complex ideas, learn continuously, and view technology from the perspective of the people who use it.

Today, I am building my career in Media Systems because it naturally brings together many of the technologies that have fascinated me throughout my life. Modern media systems sit at the intersection of networking, software, control, streaming, audio, video, cloud services, automation, and user experience. I enjoy understanding how these technologies interact as complete systems rather than as isolated components, and I look forward to continuing to learn, build, and contribute as the industry evolves.

Vision

What interests me is how audio, video, networking, streaming, software, control systems, and emerging technologies come together as one connected ecosystem. I don't think of these as separate skills to collect. I see them as different parts of the same picture, and I'm fascinated by understanding how they connect and actually work together.

My path began with Computer Science and Education, and today I'm building my career in Media Systems while continuing to explore the broader technology landscape. I know I'll never stop learning, because curiosity has always been the strongest driving force behind my journey.
